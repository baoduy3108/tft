Is it really free and private?

Yes. Compression happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to any server, so there are no size limits and it even works offline.

Which format gives the smallest file?

WebP usually produces the smallest files at the same visual quality. Use "Keep original" if you need to preserve the format for compatibility.

Why is my PNG not much smaller?

PNG is lossless, so the "quality" slider has little effect. For big savings on PNGs, convert to WebP or set a smaller max width.

Can I compress many images at once?

Yes — drop as many as you like, hit Compress, then Download all.
